 Sweet Treats for your dog

Bones

Bones have been a classic food for dogs since the old ages. 
They are full of calcium and nutrients and can be very benifitial to a dogs diet. 
Whole chickens or chicken portions, whole rabbits, and meaty beef or lamb ribs are popular ways to feed bones to dogs.
Dangerous Food for Dogs -
A number of common human foods and household ingestibles are toxic to dog.
These include chocolate solids (theobromine poisoning), onion and garlic (thiosulfate, sulfoxide or disulfide poisoning).
Grapes and raisins (cause kidney failure in dogs), milk (some dogs are lactose intolerant and suffer diarrhea, goats' milk can be beneficial).
Nutmeg (neurotoxic to dogs), macadamia nuts, as well as various plants and other potentially ingested materials.
